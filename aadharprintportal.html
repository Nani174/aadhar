Aadhaar Print Portal Works

You can print and retrieve your documents using AADHAAR PRINT PORTAL.


  1. 1. Visit Aadhaar Print Portal
    Start by visiting our website: http://aadharprintportal.info/


  2. 2. Enter the Document Number
    This could be your strong>Aadhar Number/strong> or strong>PAN number/strong>. It could be your Aadhar #, Pan number or vehicle RC#.


  3. 3. KYC verification
    Aadhaar Print Portal verifies your identity through a KYC process. Third-party API providers perform this verification, making sure that your data is secure and conforms to regulations.


  4. 4. Retrieve document information
    The portal then retrieves your ID (name), address (address) and birth date after KYC.


  5. 5. You can print or reapply your document
    With the information you’ve obtained, you can either apply for a brand new document, or you can print your existing document. Downloading the document from the portal allows you to easily print the information at your home or other locations.


What is Print Portal? Step by step guide

The Printer Portal offers a fast and simple way to find lost documents. The following is a detailed guide to how the Print Portal works.



  1. 1. Print Portal Access Website
    Start by visiting the Printer Portal site from your PC or mobile.


  2. 2. Please enter your document number
    The portal will have an entry box for your document’s number. It could be your Aadhar Number. Pan Card number. RC number.


  3. 3. KYC verification process
    After you have entered your document’s number, the Print Portal launches a KYC Verification. The process ensures that only authorized users are able to retrieve document information. Third-party APIs are used by the portal for accurate and secure verification.


  4. 4. Retrieve Document Details
    Printer Portal after successfully verifying the document will show essential details such as your name address and date of birth.


  5. 5. Request for Document
    By using these details, you may now reapply to get your missing document. Print portal contains links or instructions to help you reapply for a duplicating document. According to the type of duplicate document, you will be taken to an official website where you can finish the application process.


  6. 6. Printing the Document
    Download and Print the Document Directly from the Portal.
